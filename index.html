About
Scribl was started by Chase Miller in Dr. Michelle Meyer's lab at Boston College as an attempt to make bioinformatic charts as easy to generate as pie charts. Scribl is built on top of the HTML5 canvas element, which allows it to be very performant and to visualize large data sets.
